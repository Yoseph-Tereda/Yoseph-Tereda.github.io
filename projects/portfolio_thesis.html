Numerical Methods

  • Time integrators compared: Forward Euler (FE), Explicit Midpoint (EMP), Classical RK4, RKC(2,1), RKC(3,1), SSP(3,3), SSP(4,3).
  • Spatial / time discretization: Finite element / finite-difference approaches used for the monodomain PDE.
  • Stability analysis: The theoretical largest stable timestep is obtained by mapping Jacobian eigenvalues into the stability region of each integrator.
